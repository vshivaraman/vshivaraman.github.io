V Shivaraman

I am a predoctoral research assistant at the Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Lab (AIRL) at the Indian Institute of Science Bangalore (IISc Bangalore), headed by Dr. Suresh Sundaram.

I received my MS and BS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Bhopal (IISER Bhopal).

Currently, my research interest lies in developing robust learning-based techniques for behavior/motion prediction and planning for Autonomous Driving in Urban Environments.
